You need to click the 
colorful bubbles to pop it.
Press Space Key to pause or continue.
You will be given three hearts.
DO NOT click bubbles with bombs!
Once clicked, you will lose one heart.
If you have missed 20 bubbles
or have lost all of your hearts,
then you fail. After failed,
you can choose to play again or exit.
Your name will be used to identify 
and find your previous records.
